How to play

BETTING

You can bet on the contract by sending the ETH to the smart contract address. The maximum and minimum amount that a user can bet for is specified alongside the contract.

PAY-OFFs

Pay-offs are decided by the settlement price of Ethereum. On winning the bet, the better shall receive WEI ( 10-18 Ethereum), rounded to the nearest smallest integer, equalling contract multiplier times the amount that the better had bet for i.e. bet-size of the user.

SETTLEMENT PRICE

Settlement price shall be taken as the VWAP (volume weighted average price) of ETH/USDT, observed between Indian Standard Time 17:00 hrs and 19:00 hrs on February 9, 2018 on Poloniex Exchange. The following API shall be used for determining the settlement price of ETH/USDT: Poloniex Api

TRANSFER USING METAMASK

Clicking the ‘BET’ button shall invoke metamask, using which you can transfer ETHs to the smart contract. We do not store your private keys and this site does not relay your private key on the internet. Currently Metamask is the only way to bet on the above listed contracts. You can learn more about Metamask here: Metamask

WHAT ARE ODDS

Odds, as defined here, reflect the probability of an event. If you think that the event is more likely than the odds that are listed here, you can bet the contract. Should you be of the opinion that the odds are richer than they ought to be, you can bet on the inverse contact. Together both the events are exhaustive.

You need to install metamask chrome plugin and login using your ether wallet to play.
